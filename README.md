# HR-Attrition-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and departmental performance within an organization. The dashboard provides HR teams and business stakeholders with actionable insights to understand employee turnover patterns, improve retention strategies, and support workforce planning.

## Key Features

* Employee attrition analysis by department, gender, age group, and job role
* Workforce demographic and salary distribution insights
* Job satisfaction and work-life balance analysis
* Employee performance and experience tracking
* Attrition trend monitoring across different business units
* Interactive filters and drill-down reporting

## KPIs Included

* Total Employees
* Total Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Average Employee Age
* Average Monthly Income
* Job Satisfaction Score
* Average Years at Company

## Tools & Technologies

* Excel
* DAX
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Business Intelligence Reporting

## Business Impact

This dashboard helps organizations identify key factors contributing to employee turnover, monitor workforce trends, improve employee retention strategies, and support data-driven HR decision-making through interactive analytics and KPI tracking.
